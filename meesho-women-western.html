Different Types Of Ladies’ Sweater Outfits You Need To Try This Winter
When the winter season arrives, it's time to upgrade your wardrobe. Let go of your sleeveless tops and summer dresses and embrace comfort and style with cozy sweaters. Who wouldn't want to wear a snuggly cosy sweater with a cup of coffee on a foggy winter day? With winter fashion coming up with new designs of jackets and coats for women, the charm of the classic sweater seemed to have waned over time. This season, women’s sweaters are set to make a grand comeback in a new and improved avatar. Check out some of the latest sweater looks for ladies that are making the rounds on the runways.
Knit Sweater With Jeggings
Knit sweaters reek of class and sophistication. Be it a V-neck, round neck, or drop shoulder design, the texture remains one of the most fashionable choices. Depending on your personal style, choose a colour that stands out and looks luxurious and rich. Most high-end brands are coming up with neutral shades that are appropriate for all occasions and go with multiple outfits. Wear a drop shoulder, oversized knit sweater with fitted jeggings in navy blue or black. This look goes best with flat boots in black or brown. To add even more oomph to this look choose thigh-high boots. If the sweater is muted and subtle, add a colourful knit muffler and a matching cap to add a pop of color.
Woollen Cardigan With Dress
This has to be the oldest and most classic sweater design in existence. Cardigans are one of the most versatile winter wear pieces that you can have. Even though you can wear a cardigan with almost any outfit, there is no match for the cardigan-with-dress look. If you wear a plain dress, go for a printed, well-fitted cardigan that reaches your waist. For a printed dress, wear a plain cardigan in a colour picked out from the dress pattern. If you want to wear it with a form-fitting dress, or for a loger, jacket style cardigan in a neutral color. Wear woollen stockings to keep your legs warm and opt for high-heralded calf-length boots.
Classic Turtleneck Sweater With A-line Skirt
The basic design of a turtleneck sweater has a high neck which goes up to the chin. This is one of the designs that were influenced by western fashion and entered India only in the last few decades. This sweater will keep you warm and make you look stylish. To ace, this look, go for a plain turtleneck in a neutral hue and pair it with a calf-length A-line skirt, also in a neutral colour. To amp up the look, wear knee-high boots. You can also wear a trench coat over the outfit for extra warmth and comfort.
Hoodie With Jeans
This is the go-to winter look for most college girls. There is no match for the comfort and cosiness it provides. Whether you want to wear a plain, printed, or themed hoodie, just throw it on over whatever top you are wearing and pair it with your favourite jeans. The oversized hoodie worn with a pair of jeggings looks quite stylish and the look is super popular these days. As for footwear, you can simply wear sneakers or combat boots to add a sporty and slightly edgy touch to your outfit.
Woollen Pullover Sweaters For Women
Pullovers are simply sweaters that do not have buttons or zips and can be worn over the head. This is a unisex design loved by both men and women. You can wear a printed pullover with denim for a casual look. For a sophisticated office look, throw on a woollen overcoat or a blazer. You can wear pullovers with jeans or tailored trousers. Put on block heeled shoes to complement this look. If you are wearing jeans, you can wear sneakers or sports shoes as well for a comfortable casual look.
Cropped Sweater With Plain Tshirt
Cropped sweaters have only just entered the fashion scene. As the name suggests, this is a contemporary variation of the classic sweater design. The only difference is that the length is cropped to make it shorter. Typically these sweaters are layered over a woolen top or T-shirt. You can get a button-down cardigan style design or a pullover style V neck design. If you want to experiment a little go for a wrap style sweater. Wear it with a plain white or black top underneath and pair it with skinny jeans and ankle boots for the perfect winter look.
Poncho Sweaters For Ladies
When we talk about comfort, a Poncho sweater is what strikes the mind immediately. Ponchos have been worn by people in the hilly regions of India for many years. They are traditionally made of very thick pure woolen fabric tailored into a design that covers the upper body including the arms and waist. Designers have revived this traditional garment and now, celebrities and fashion divas are sporting Ponchos everywhere. They may not be appropriate for the office but if you want to make a stylish statement while staying super comfortable in the winter, throw on a Poncho sweater over your regular outfit. They come in exotic prints and patterns so you have a huge array of options.
