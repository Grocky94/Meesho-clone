All About The Latest Kids’ Dress Designs For Your Baby Girl
These days, people put a whole lot of effort into choosing the right, stylish outfits for their kids. In fact, designers often launch separate kidswear collections every season for kids who want to start their fashion game early. Just like you, your baby girl also needs to have a separate collection of casual and occasional wear outfits. Right from the colors to the designs to the fits, everything needs to be approved by your kid of course. But before you select her brand new wardrobe, you need to know about the latest trends that are ruling kids’ fashion these days. So take a look at the following list to find out all you need to know about dressing up your kid the right way.
Printed A-Line Kid’s Dresses
Ever since kids’ fashion trends began to be taken seriously, there were so many innovative designs and cuts entering the market every season. But one classic dress design managed to retain its popularity throughout a;l the new changes. The simple A-line dress can be worn by kids, teenagers, and older women, making it one of the most versatile dress designs in the market. Pair the dress with cute ballerina shoes and frilly socks for the most adorable look for your little princess. Add a printed hairband or hair clips in colorful patterns to add an extra dose of cuteness.
Pastel Pinafore Kids Dresses
The evergreen outfit for Indian girls for the summer is the adorable pinafore dress. These are usually made of light cotton fabric and come in cute prints and fun colors. However, there is no match for pastel-colored pinafore dresses. Go for pale pinks, light blues, and lemon yellows this season. The dresses usually come with pockets where your little girl can put her precious belongings without any fear of losing them. Your girl will definitely stay comfortable and look trendy in these lovely dresses.
Layered Casual Kids’ Dresses
Another popular kids’ dress design is a layered dress. This one is a stunning and smart outfit that your kid can wear at home or to a party. For a fancy look, pick a design with bows and fringes. But even the basic layered dress which has two more colorful fabrics overlapping each other is great for a casual look. To dress it up a little, you can add funky accessories and footwear that go well with the dress. A floral print design adorned with colorful flowers in bright and bubbly colors would look adorable on your little princess.
Kids’ Bubble Dresses
Looking for a unique and modern dress for your baby girl’s birthday? We have just what you need. Get a cute little bubble dress. The name refers to the hem of this dress design which has a fluffy bubble-like look. These dresses usually come with pockets that will help your kid keep her tiny belongings safe. Go for bright colors and cute prints to make her stand out even more. A bright pink bubble dress with strappy shoes and a small sling bag is the perfect party look.
Frilled Kids’ Dresses
This dress design has been a kids’ favorite for many years now and there is no way this is getting old anytime soon. The bodice of this dress design is usually made of glossy fabrics like satin and the lower part is made of net. Soft net ruffles and frills add a funky look to the dress. Some designs come with a belt at the waist with can be tied up like a bow to add a fun vibe. Your child will look just like her favorite Disney princess in one of these dresses.
Popular Patterns And Fabrics For Kids’ Dresses
It may be easy to find the right type of dress for your girl. But there is a lot more to it than that. You need to make sure that the pattern appeals to your kid and the fabric is comfortable to wear. Cotton, Linen, Muslin, and fleece along with some soft blended fabrics are the best in terms of durability and comfort. For party wear dresses, net and velvet are also good options. However, when it comes to fabric its best to stick to what your child seems to be the most comfortable in. As for patterns, you can get as creative as you want with this aspect. In fact, you should go for patterns that your child likes and if possible, let them make the final choice. Floral prints, animal motifs as well as TV and movie-inspired prints have been popular for quite some time. You can also get some traditional patterns and prints, but do make sure that the colors are bright and vibrant. Dull colors rarely appeal to little kids.
How To Style Kids’ Dresses Perfectly?
Accessories: You simply cannot ignore accessories when choosing the right outfit for your kid. Colorful headbands, clips, rubber bands that match the color and theme of the outfit are a must. Next comes jewelry! Now, kids’ jewelry designs are usually limited to safe, unbreakable materials and cute, funky designs. Get some bracelets, watches, stud earrings, and anklets for your girl’s accessory collection.
Footwear: You will find just as much variety in kids’ footwear as that for grown-ups. From cute ballerinas to funky flip-flops and even combat boots, you have all the options you need. While matching your kids’ footwear to the outfit is important, it is comfort that matters the most when it comes to footwear. So choose comfy shoes that your kid prefers. As for designs, there are so many to pick from. A pair of printed sneakers, embellished bellies, ankle boots, and of course slip-on shoes must be added to your collection.
Hairstyles: Pigtails and plaits have had their day! Modern kids need to be on par with current hair trends. You can go for a simple hairstyle like a high pony if your girl has long or medium length hair. Add funky rubber bands and clips to make her stand out. When you plan on leaving her hair down, add a nice colorful hairband to enhance her cuteness to the next level.
