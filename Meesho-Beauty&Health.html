More About Face Makeup

If you are new to the world of face makeup, start with the basics- primer, foundation, concealer, and then move on to contour and blush.

Best Face Makeup Products That Every Girl Should Have
When it comes to face makeup, there is a thin line between the flawless finish and a complete disaster. To avoid the latter, it’s important that you get the right products and apply them in the right way. Face makeup includes four broad steps- base makeup, eye makeup, lip makeup, and contouring. Finding the right face makeup products is not easy, whether you are a makeup junkie or an amateur. So take a look at our list of the most essential makeup products that all women should have. All of these products are available in different varieties and finish. So pick the one that suits your skin tone and type.
Must-Have Face Makeup Products For Women
Primer
If you are looking for a flawless finish for your makeup and want it to stay in place all day. You must invest in a good quality primer. We often overlook the importance of getting the right base for your makeup. Using a primer can help all other products to apply smoothly and stay on longer. If you have large pores, a primer will help cover them and give your skin a smoother look. You can also use a color-correcting primer if you have any redness on your skin. Some primers are tinted so they can provide a bit of coverage. If you feel like you do not need much coverage, get a tinted primer to save yourself the hassle of buying a foundation or BB cream.
Concealer
The primary goal of using a concealer is to hide any flaws on your skin. It can be used on blemishes, scars, discoloration, freckles, dark circles, or anything that makes your skin look less than perfect. If you have any skin issues that you want to cover, a concealer is a must-have makeup product for you. You need to choose the right texture and finish for your concealer which is specifically formulated to target your issues. You also need to take your skin type into account and select a product that is right for your skin and gives you the right level of coverage as well.
Foundation
A foundation is one of the most important face makeup products and needs to be chosen carefully. Unlike a concealer, foundation need not be used only on the problem areas of your skin. You can apply a thin layer all over your face and neck to even out your skin tone and hide imperfections. It needs to be applied after the primer and concealer and before your contouring and eye/lip makeup. They are available in different coverage levels and in liquid, powder, and mousse or cream forms so you can select one as per your needs and skin type. Make sure your foundation shade matches your skin tone perfectly or your makeup will end up looking unnatural and cakey. Use a damp makeup sponge or brush to make sure your foundation blends smoothly.
Contouring/Highlighting Products
For makeup newbies, contouring and highlighting may seem intimidating. But it is not as difficult as it seems. By using the right products, you can enhance your best features and give your face the perfect chiseled look. For contouring, you need a bronzer which should be a couple of shades darker than your skin tone. Use it on the edges of your face such as the hairline, under your cheekbones, and the jawline. As for the highlighter it does the opposite of what a bronzer does. It highlights the areas of your face on which light falls directly. The bridge of your nose, chin, and cheekbones are the places where you use a highlighter. This will give your skin a luminous glow instantly.
Eye Makeup
Eye makeup products are what most of us start out with. A simple Kajal or eyeliner can instantly uplift your eyes. You can get different types of eye makeup products like eyeliners, Kohl, Mascara, eyeshadow, etc. If you have oily skin, use powder, and gel-based eye products. For dry skin, liquid and cream formulations work best. Depending on your preference, you can pick from a range of different shades in all of these products. Try out some of the latest makeup trends like cat eyeliner and winged eyeliner or the smoky eye look for more emphasis. When you are doing your eye makeup, it is important to consider your eyebrows as well. You can use an eyebrow pencil or powder to give them the shape you want.
Lip Colors
Lip makeup is where most women shine. This is one of the most popular sections of makeup products and even those who are not that into face makeup usually have a small collection of lip colors. This is also the category where you will find the most variety in terms of shades and finish. If you want a long-lasting color, go for matte or liquid lip colors and if you like a dewy, plump look, go for glossy or cream finish lipsticks. If you just need a hint of color on your lips, get a few subtle lip tints or lip stains. A lip liner is another must-have product, especially if you have thin lips. You can use this to outline the edges of your lips before filling them in with lip colors.
