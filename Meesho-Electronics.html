Best Design Ladies Bags That You Must Have In Your Wardrobe

You can never have enough handbags, the more you own, the lesser it is. Girls, middle-aged women, old age women everybody loves bags. Whenever you see in a movie, fashion magazine or your favorite celebrity, that bag is all you want for your collection, isn’t it? Bags can be for your office, party, or any day outing. There are different types of ladies’ bags or handbags that you can select for any outfit or occasion.



Leather Bags -
Leather bags are always in and never go out of style. Many styles will come and go but these bags will always shine. They are great for any occasion. They have a unique elegance about them and are available in many colors, just for you. They are easily available online at Meesho in any size and shape.
Backpack -
A backpack is always comfortable and very easy to carry. You can select a stylish backpack for everyday outing or travel backpack. Backpack also comes in small, medium, large, very large sizes. You can select one according to your need.
Designer Jute Bags -
Jute bags are made up of jute. They are very unique and different styles are available like fancy jute bags, stylish jute bags, simple jute bags. They have amazing strong texture and the best style is spiral round bags don’t wait to get them.
Clutch -
Clutch is a small handy bag for when you want to carry something light, then must go with a clutch. They are light weighted, small and can be easily held in your hand. They are best for a night full of dancing and fun. You can keep your phone, cards, makeup and you will be good to go.
Tote bag -
Tote bags are big enough to carry all of your stuff. They come in vibrant colors and exquisite designs and are perfectly subtle and classy for girls. These are perfect for those beach walks and picnics but can also be paired with a stylish dress for a fun casual look.
Shoulder bag -
Shoulder bag is also big and classy like a tote bag but it has more compartments. This makes it easy for you to organize your stuff inside your shoulder bag nicely. They can also be used to carry your laptop and books as well.
Quilted bag -
These are the iconic bags that you can see your favorite celebrities holding all various events. We all drool for them. So what are you waiting for? Get your hands on one of these beauties as soon as you can.
Sling bags -
Slings bags never run out of fashion and suit all body types and all ages. Also, because of the sling attached, these are much easier to carry and there is less chance of you losing your bag.It can be very chic for girls and very elegant for women. You can get an envelope clutch or metal clutch.



How To Style Your Stylish Bags With Different Outfit



Party/ Cocktail -
Are you getting decked up for a party? Rock your gorgeous outfit with clutches and miniature bags as they are ideal for this type of event. You can get studded and heavily embellished designs to add more oomph to your look.
Formal Ladies Bags–
For formal wear try some shoulder or tote bag that is big enough to carry all your stuff that you need to carry with you like your laptop, wallet, makeup pouch, etc. Select those colors that compliment your formal attire like black, white, natural shade, etc.
Picnic –
Planning to go for a picnic? It is best to take a medium-sized funky backpack with you. While getting ready for trekking, it is best to go with a trekking backpack but do not forget to take your trekking shoe.
Shopping –
If you are going to a flea market, wear a boho dress and pair it with colorful and vibrant jute bags. So, you can carry your purchased product in that. Style your outfit with some Kolhapuri chappal and you are good to go.
Day Out Bags –
If you are going for a day out with your friends and can't decide what to wear? A floral dress and a printed small sling bag is your best friend.. Take a chic sling bag and pair it with casual shoes or plum sandals. This will make you look cool, stylish and fashionable in no time.
